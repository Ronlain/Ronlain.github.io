Zoombies

Project Summary

Bike your way though a post-apocalyptic wasteland full of zombies and death! Will you deliver the cure, or will humanity fall? Find out in this VR biking experience.

In Zoombies the player is controlled by the VirZoom and the HTC Vive headset. They traverse through levels by pedaling on the bike an dodging zombies as well as obstacles on their way to the end of each level.

Zoombies was developed by a team of students at Becker College as a part of thier "Game Studio" course.

I joined the team in the fall of 2017. Our goal was to optimize and streamline level 1 and our tutorial by the end of the semester.

My Contributions

  • Created the player controller using the VirZoom SDK
  • Created temporary main menu UI and implemented in-game UI
