Project Summary

Inner Demon began development in the Summer of 2019 through an internship provided by MassDiGI. A team of 5 was selected via an application process and then put to work on a project that was intended to be entered into student game competitions.

Inner Demon is designed as a 2.5D puzzle platformer that incorportates both platforming and combat in a unique way while also providing the player with high quality visuals.

I began work on Inner Demon in the Summer of 2019 as part of the previously mentioned MassDiGI internship. We had many ideas for games based on games that had won student game competitions in the past, but in the end we decided that Inner Demon was going to be the easiest to work on while still giving us the best chances of performing well at these competitions. My work on Inner Demon spanned the 12 weeek period in the Summer and then continued into the fall semester as the game was brought into Becker College's Live Studio course to be worked on. There I became the lead programmer on the project.

My Contributions

  • Created the character controller in the summer and was responsible for working with designers to provide a smooth gameplay experience
  • Reworked the AI system into a finite state machine, bringing ease of use to designers and to reduce technical debt
  • Implementation of various UI features working in tandem with artists
